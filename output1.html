alnyea
5.0 out of 5 stars So far so good!
Reviewed in the United States on August 16, 2019
Size: TRAVELVerified Purchase
I got this hair dryer primarily for using at home, although I do realize its a travel dryer. Full-sized dryers tend to be too big and heavy for me, and this product is the perfect size and weight. I was worried that it might be a compact size like other travel dryers, but Its more of what Id call medium sized.

Some more pros:
- The folding feature allows it to be neatly stored without taking up too much space.
- I LOVE the multiple settings: cool, warm, hot for temperature and off, low, high for power. I use it on a warm on high setting according to the instructions that came in the box, and am able to style my hair perfectly every time.
- This dryer is supposed to adjust the voltage automatically so you dont have to. (Ive only used it on 110v so far and as soon as I turned it on the first time, the light on 110v turned on as it automatically detected it.)

Cons:
- Theres really only one con Ive found so far, which is that the concentrator nozzle fits very tight and therefore is really hard to turn. I dry my hair with the nozzle turned vertically, but style with it turned horizontally. Every time I try to turn it, I have to turn the dryer off and actually exert some force. Im hoping it gets easier with repeated use.
